Frequently asked

Where in the world will Dr. Rizvi travel?

Globally. Dr. Rizvi has spoken and trained on three continents — North America, Europe and Asia (from the Middle East to China) — and accepts virtual, hybrid and in-person engagements. Travel from Vancouver, BC.

What does a typical training engagement look like?

From a 60-minute keynote to a four-day immersive workshop, all curricula are tailored to your industry, audience seniority and target outcomes. Multi-cohort programmes (e.g. an annual leadership academy) are designed with your L&D team and delivered alongside ongoing coaching.

Does Dr. Rizvi coach individuals as well as teams?

Yes. Confidential 1:1 coaching is offered to C-suite leaders, high-potential talent and certification candidates (PMP, CAPM, CBAP, PMI-ACP, PMI-RMP, PMI-PBA, Lean Six Sigma, SAFe, Scrum, ITIL and others). Engagements typically run 3–12 months.

What is the lead time for booking?

Two business days for a tailored proposal. Most keynotes and workshops are confirmed 6–12 weeks out; multi-month coaching and advisory engagements are scheduled further ahead. Urgent / replacement-speaker requests are considered when the calendar permits.

How are fees structured?

Fees depend on format, audience size, preparation required and travel. Indicative ranges are captured in the enquiry form; Dr. Rizvi's office will confirm a fixed quote — including any travel and licencing costs — within the proposal.

Can the team have a copy of Dr. Rizvi's bio and headshot?

Yes — the press kit below contains an approved short bio, long bio, headshots in print and web resolution, and a high-resolution version of the speaker one-sheet. Mention "Press / Media" in the enquiry form for additional assets (intro reel, custom topic abstracts, etc.).

Does Dr. Rizvi accept podcast and media interviews?

Selectively, yes — particularly on leadership, agile transformation, AI in project delivery, risk management and the future of work. Please use the enquiry form and choose "Media, podcast or panel."
