About

A scholar practitioner shaping the next generation of leaders.

Dr. S. Hasnain Rizvi is an acclaimed public speaker, mentor, executive coach and author whose career spans complex programs for a Canadian Crown Corporation, the Government of Newfoundland and Labrador, and Amdocs, where he led a major Business Transformation Programme. He also spent roughly a decade in California leading initiatives for Fortune 500 companies.

Today he serves as an adjunct instructor at the UBC Sauder School of Business, the University of California Irvine, UC Riverside, SAIT Polytechnic, LATTC and ITMPI, teaching the Certificate in Business Analysis, the Certificate in Agile Leadership and related graduate programmes. He is a sought-after seminar leader for Leoron, NetCom Learning, Pluralsight and QuickStart, and a senior judge of the AI World Series, the British Computer Society Fellowship panel, the IIBA Exam Global Committee and the annual International Business Excellence Awards in Dubai.

In 2019 he completed his doctorate at SKEMA Business School with research focused on Agile project and program management methodologies. His most recent engagements bring that work to bear on enterprise agile transformations, AI-augmented project delivery, business intelligence and high-performance teaming, including extensive coaching work in the insurance sector.
